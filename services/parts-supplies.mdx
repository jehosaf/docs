## Adhesive Cold Bonding Applicator Hoses

Our adhesive hoses are built for the rigors of professional roofing work and designed to deliver consistent, reliable adhesive flow throughout the life of the hose.

- **Custom sizes to meet specific job requirements** — Not all adhesive machines and application setups are identical. We fabricate custom-length and custom-configuration hoses to fit your specific equipment, ensuring a proper fit without improvised workarounds.
- **Standard sizes in stock** — Common hose lengths and configurations are available for immediate purchase, so you can replace a worn or damaged hose without waiting on a special order.
- **Designed for efficient adhesive application** — Our hoses are selected and built for smooth, consistent flow that supports clean, uniform adhesive coverage — reducing waste and improving the quality of your bonding work.
- **Compatible with common adhesive machine brands** — Whether you're running a standard cold-process adhesive applicator or a specialized machine, we can match or build a hose to fit your system.
