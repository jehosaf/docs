## Keeping Your Fleet Moving

The most expensive repair is the one you didn't see coming. Proactive, scheduled maintenance is the single best way to protect your investment in your fleet and avoid the costly breakdowns that sideline a truck for days at a time. A missed oil change, worn brake pads, or a slow coolant leak can each escalate into a major repair — or worse, leave one of your drivers stranded on the side of the road. At Josias Auto Service, we help you stay ahead of those problems with thorough inspections and honest recommendations every time your truck comes in. We treat your fleet the way you'd want your own vehicle treated.

<Note>
  We service both gas and diesel trucks. Contact us with your vehicle's year, make, model, and mileage for a repair or maintenance estimate tailored to your fleet.
</Note>
